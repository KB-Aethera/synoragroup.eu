Synora derives the foundations autonomous intelligence requires.

Every claim currently made about autonomous intelligent systems rests on assumptions that have not been formally established. The field is building upward. The ground floor does not exist.

What follows from this is not a better architecture. It is the necessary conditions themselves — derived, not designed. From them emerges NOUS: the first class of weightless AI systems whose intelligence is a structural consequence of geometry, not a product of training — scaling from a medical sensor to a drone swarm by changing a single parameter, genuinely autonomous because the structure of the problem requires it.
